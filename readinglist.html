  • The Talent Code - Daniel Coyle
    • - This book goes deep into the foundations of learning and what goes into reaching peak performance. This is a great book for understanding the processes for learning any skill, not just guitar.
  • Little Book of Talent
    • - Built on the principles of The Talent Code, The Little Book of Talent is a handbook full of actionable tips for learning skills. It is plenty useful on its own!
  • The Music Lesson - Victor Wooten
    • - Wisdom and music lessons intertwine in this book on musicianship written by one of the greatest musicians and music teacher of our time
    Mastery - Robert Greene - A methodical look at what it takes to become a master at something. Part historical case studies of people like Da Vinci, part a look into the science of attention and learning
  • Effortless Mastery - Kenny Werner
    • - A book for musicians by a jazz pianist. It is devoted to creating a healthy psychology toward one’s playing. Essential reading for anyone aspiring to make a living playing music!
  • Zen guitar - Philip Toshio Sudo
    • - Guitar as seen through the timeless wisdom of Zen. A book where you can pick up and read any page and find something profound to reflect upon. Filled with perspective-shifting quotes from famous musicians, and advice rarely written about dozens of niche topics
  • Mindset - Dr. Carol Dweck
    • - Dr. Dweck’s research into mindset has been nothing short of paradigm-shifting. This book is the culmination of her years of research into the subject. Essential reading for anyone looking to reach their fullest potential. At the very least, watch her TED talk.
  • Supernatural Strategies For Starting a Rock n Roll Group
    • A humorous yet academic and pragmatic handbook for navigating the many trappings of band membership. Essential for anyone with a creative vision to start their own band
