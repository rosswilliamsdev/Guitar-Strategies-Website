Whether you're learning guitar for the first time or an experienced musician, the strategies and information presented here will increase your skill level and knowledge base. The only conditions are consistency and attention to detail. Let's define what these terms mean:

Consistency is:

  • Playing the same things
  • The same way
  • Everytime you play

Attention to detail is:

  • Playing the right notes
  • articulations
  • and rhythms
  • with the right fingerings
  • in the right position

As you grow as a musician, your listening skills will improve dramatically. You'll hear every sound around you, not just music, with more clarity. You'll also learn to notice the relationships between sounds and how they function in different contexts. This means as you develop your skills, new levels of sonic detail will reveal themselves to you. Consider it a calling to express these new sounds on your instrument.
